What's a Code-a-thon?

It's a beautiful thing that happens when people come together to create code. Young, curious souls, just like you, sign up individually or as a team to compete in the event, where you have 36-hours to complete a theme-related application or game.

Code

Design and code your project in 36-hours with a team of your friends around a silly theme that's chosen for the weekend. You choose the project, framework and scope of the idea. Your code doesn't have to be beautiful, just functional.

Learn

Don't know where to begin? Ask a mentor for feedback. They'll give you recommendations on how to structure your code, frameworks you could use, game ideas, and more. Programmers of all skill-levels are welcome.

Win

At the end of the weekend, everyone's submissions are judged and a prize is given to the winner. Even if your project doesn't win, you can still get a door prize. And we'll have free pizza, which means everyone's a winner.
