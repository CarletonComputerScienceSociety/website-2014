  Resources  

Free CS and Programming Books!

Check out this list before you head down to Chapters or the Bookstore. It has all sorts of great reading material for Computer Science students! Everything from algorithms and discrete math texts to language texts from Ada to xBase is there for you.

Free Software

Didn't know you get free software as a member of Carleton Computer Science? You do now! Head on over the SCS Dreamspark page and log in to get access to tons of programs. Visual Studio, Visio, SQL Server, and all kinds of versions of Windows are only some of the highlights.

Git Tutorials

Time to git gud. Version control is a critical skill in Computer Science and nothing will help you polish your skills with one of the best tools around like this set of Atlassian tutorials. Get your git skills up to speed or just refresh yourself on that one command you need to clean up the mess you just made.

Discrete Math Study Center

COMP 1805 getting you down? Rise up to the challenge and hone your skills right here. It won't replace those lectures you skipped but it will help you get back on your feet. Worried about a test? Study up quick!

SCS Job Postings

Check out career opportunities posted by the School of Computer Science. They might be right up your alley!

Important Dates and Deadlines

Here's a quick link to all of those critical dates during your school year at Carleton University. Check it out so you don't get surpised by anything the school is throwing your way.
