  Resources  

Free CS and Programming Books!

Check out this list before you head down to Chapters or the Bookstore. It has all sorts of great reading material for Computer Science students! Everything from algorithms and discrete math texts to language texts from Ada to xBase is there for you.

Free Software

Didn't know you get free software as a member of Carleton Computer Science? You do now! Head on over the SCS Dreamspark page and log in to get access to tons of programs. Visual Studio, Visio, SQL Server, and all kinds of versions of Windows are only some of the highlights.

Git Tutorials

Time to git gud. Version control is a critical skill in Computer Science and nothing will help you polish your skills with one of the best tools around like this set of Atlassian tutorials. Get your git skills up to speed or just refresh yourself on that one command you need to clean up the mess you just made.

Discrete Math Study Center

COMP 1805 getting you down? Rise up to the challenge and hone your skills right here. It won't replace those lectures you skipped but it will help you get back on your feet. Worried about a test? Study up quick!

SCS Job Postings

Check out career opportunities posted by the School of Computer Science. They might be right up your alley!

Important Dates and Deadlines

Here's a quick link to all of those critical dates during your school year at Carleton University. Check it out so you don't get surpised by anything the school is throwing your way.

  Get Involved  

Volunteer!

Everything the Carleton Computer Science Society does is run entirely by volunteers. If you want to get involved and help out your fellow students or work to see the CCSS do things that you want to see then this is the best way to get that happening! More Info

CCSS Events

The CCSS runs lots of events during the fall and winter semesters, so come check them out. You'll find your peers enjoying the same things and occasionally your profs having a great time too! They're a great way to relax away the stress of school. More Info

Committees

Most, if not all, of what the CCSS does is managed by a variety of committees staffed by our Directors and membership. If you'd like to take action in guiding the management and development of what the CCSS has to offer then this is the best way to do it! More Info

Society Meetings

Where the coordination between our Directors, members, and committees all happens. Attend meetings to get a great sense of what the CCSS is up to, vote on important society issues, and meet up with committees! More Info

Partnered Clubs (not sure if this belongs here)

Got a club that represents the interests and hobbies of CCSS students? Get into contact with the us to work on joint ventures, sponsorship, advertising, and more. If you're a CS student looking for clubs then take a look at our list of partnered clubs! More Info

Partnership

Looking to present to Comp. Sci. students, help them out, entertain them, or let them know you exist? Contact us for a joint event, promotion, or other potential service! We love supporting everything that supports our members. More Info
