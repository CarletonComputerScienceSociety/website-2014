CCSS General Election 2015/2016

All CCSS full members may vote in this election (Undergraduate students enrolled in a Computer Science degree program at Carleton University). Polling opens at 00:00h on Thursday, March 24th, 2016 and closes at 23:59h on Saturday, March 26th, 2016.

The Electoral System

This election will run by direct ballot. This means for every position up for election you may select either one (1) or zero (0) candidates from the list of candidates running that position. Selection of zero (0) candidates will be determined as an abstaining vote. Additionally, you may select for any position a vote of no confidence, which will be counted as a vote that no running candidate is suitable for the position.

Voting is anonymous. You will be asked to log in to an authentication service run by the School of Computer Science to verify that you are an eligible voter, and then returned to the CCSS voting server. The SCS does not pass on your identity.
